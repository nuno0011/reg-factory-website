· 004 · FAQ

Common Questions

What accounts do I get?+
Each mint delivers a ready-to-use account bundle including ChatGPT, Claude, and Grok — all registered under a single Outlook mailbox. Cookies are exported and accessible immediately.
How are accounts generated?+
The pipeline uses BitBrowser for unique browser fingerprints, Clash Verge for clean proxy rotation, and automated CAPTCHA solving — fully headless and unattended.
What payment do you accept?+
USDT (TRC-20). After minting, you'll receive a payment address and QR code. Once the transaction is confirmed on-chain, your account credentials are delivered automatically via the dashboard and Telegram.
Can I run this myself?+
Yes. The full source code is open on GitHub. Clone it, configure your environment, and you're running. The mint is for those who prefer a turnkey solution.
